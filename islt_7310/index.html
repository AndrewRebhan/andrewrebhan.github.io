About Me

I am proud to be an English Language Arts and Publications teacher in the Mehlville School District located in Saint Louis, MO. When I’m not teaching 11-14-year-old students at Margaret Buerkle Middle School, I’m teaching my 1-year-old son and 3-year-old daughter how to share. In my fleeting free time I enjoy designing tabletop games for use in the classroom, photography, and editing videos/images.
